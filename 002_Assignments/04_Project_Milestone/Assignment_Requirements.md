# Project Milestone Instructions and Grading Rubric

## Submission Information

**Unlimited Attempts Allowed**  
**Available:** Jan 13, 2026 12:00am - Feb 28, 2026 11:59pm

## Instructions

You are required to submit a concise milestone report that complies strictly with the following requirements:

### Format & Length

- The report must be **exactly two pages** using the provided ACM LaTeX template.
- The report must be written in **LaTeX** with no template modifications. Use the provided minimal example with the [official ACM template](https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template).
- References **do not count** toward the two-page limit.

### Required Sections

1. **Title:** Clear and descriptive.
2. **Abstract:** Problem, approach, and preliminary results (max 150 words).
3. **Introduction & Problem Statement:** Motivation, significance, and objectives.
4. **Related Work:** Relevant prior work with ACM-style citations.
5. **Methodology:** Description of techniques and evaluation metrics.
6. **Preliminary Results:** Initial findings with brief analysis.
7. **References:** Proper ACM reference style.

### Figures & Tables

- Use only what is necessary to convey key information.
- Figures must be compact, informative, and **not used as fillers**.
- All figures and tables must be **captioned and referenced in text**.

### Writing Quality

- Clear, concise, and logically organized.
- Professional tone with correct grammar and spelling.

### Submission

- Submit a **compiled PDF only**.
- Do **not** submit LaTeX source files.

## Grading Rubric (10% of Final Grade)

| Category                  | Points  | Criteria                                                                                |
| ------------------------- | ------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Technical Accuracy        | 3       | Sound methodology, appropriate metrics, and evidence-backed claims.                     |
| Depth & Relevance         | 3       | Clear problem understanding, relevant related work, and alignment with course material. |
| Clarity of Writing        | 2       | Well-structured, concise, and free of grammatical or stylistic issues.                  |
| Presentation & Compliance | 1       | Strict adherence to ACM format and effective use of figures/tables.                     |
| Referencing               | 1       | Correct and relevant ACM-style citations.                                               |
| **Total**                 | **10%** | Scaled to 100 for consistency.                                                          |

---

## Deduction Policy

- **−2 points:** Exceeding or falling short of the two-page limit (excluding references).
- **−2 points:** Non-ACM format or template modification.
- **−1 point per section:** Missing required sections.
- **−1 point:** Figures used as fillers or lacking captions/references.
- **Up to −2 points:** Poor writing quality or excessive grammatical issues.
- **−1 point:** Incorrect or missing references.

> **Note:** Submissions that do not comply with format, length, or LaTeX requirements will be penalized accordingly.

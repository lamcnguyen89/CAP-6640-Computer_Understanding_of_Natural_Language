# Final Project Report Evaluation Rubric (150 Points)

## Assignment Details

**Attempts Allowed:** Unlimited  
**Available:** Jan 13, 2026 12:00am until Apr 18, 2026 11:59pm

---

## General Submission Guidelines

- **Page Limit:** Exactly 6 pages, excluding references and appendix. Reports shorter or longer than 6 pages will be penalized (−5 points per extra or missing page).

- **Section Order:** The report must strictly follow this order:
  1. Abstract
  2. Introduction & Problem Statement
  3. Related Work
  4. Methodology
  5. Evaluation & Results
  6. Discussion & Challenges
  7. Concluding Remarks
  8. References

- **Figures & Tables:** Must be clear, relevant, and properly captioned. The Methodology section must include a pipeline figure.

- **Writing Style:** Concise, precise, and well-structured. Use subsections, paragraph headers, itemization, and enumeration where appropriate.

- **Metrics:** Must be clearly defined and presented as standalone components (not embedded inline in text).

- **Code & Artifacts:** A working link (GitHub, Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) must be provided immediately after the abstract.

- **Use of AI Tools:** The use of generative AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude) for writing, structuring, or generating any part of the report is strictly prohibited. Any violation will result in an automatic FAIL (0/150) for the project.

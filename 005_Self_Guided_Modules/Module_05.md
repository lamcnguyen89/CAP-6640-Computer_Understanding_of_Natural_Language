Introduction to Module 5
Module Introduction
Main Focus: This module examines advanced neural text generation, dialogue modeling, and decoding strategies for modern language models. It integrates neural summarization (including bottom-up and reinforcement learning approaches), conversational question answering, structured storytelling, non-autoregressive generation, and decoding control mechanisms. Students will analyze how generation quality emerges from the interaction between architecture (Seq2Seq, Transformer), objective functions (MLE vs. RL), structured planning (event-based and persona-driven models), and inference-time strategies (beam search, sampling, temperature scaling). The module emphasizes controllability, coherence, diversity, and evaluation across open-ended generation tasks.

Secondary Focus: Beyond modeling techniques, this module explores dialogue failures (genericness, repetition, inconsistency), persona and strategy modeling, state tracking in narrative generation, multimodal storytelling, and the limitations of traditional evaluation metrics such as BLEU and ROUGE. Students will compare autoregressive and non-autoregressive paradigms, study reward-based optimization, and critically assess evaluation frameworks for summarization, dialogue, and storytelling. The goal is to develop principled understanding of how to design, decode, and evaluate robust neural generation systems.

Module Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:

Explain neural natural language generation tasks and architectures.
Differentiate bottom-up, abstractive, and RL-based summarization methods.
Analyze dialogue system types and diagnose common generation failures.
Design structured and event-driven story generation pipelines.
Explain conversational QA architectures and hybrid retrieval-generation systems.
Compare autoregressive and non-autoregressive generation paradigms.
Evaluate generation quality using automatic and human-centered metrics.
Analyze the impact of decoding strategies on coherence, fluency, and diversity.
